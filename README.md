## ğŸ“– Usage

### Creating an Invoice

1. Click the **"New Invoice"** button
2. Fill in client information (name, email, address)
3. Add invoice details (number, date, due date)
4. Add line items with descriptions, hours, and rates
5. Add optional notes
6. Click **"Create Invoice"**

### Managing Invoices

- **View Details**: Click the eye icon to see full invoice information
- **Export PDF**: Click the download icon to generate a printable PDF
- **Toggle Status**: Click the status badge to mark as Paid/Unpaid
- **Export CSV**: Click "Export CSV" to download all transactions

### Theme Switching

Click the sun/moon icon in the header to toggle between dark and light themes. Your preference is automatically saved.
